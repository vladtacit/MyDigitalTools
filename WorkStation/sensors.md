```bash
sudo sensors-detect
```

The sensors-detect is an interactive command. It will walk you through scanning your system for various hardware monitoring chips installed on your motherboard.

At the end you will see message as follows on the screen:

```bash
Driver `coretemp':
  * Chip `Intel digital thermal sensor' (confidence: 9)

    Chip `Nuvoton NCT5532D/NCT6779D Super IO Sensors' (confidence: 9)
```
